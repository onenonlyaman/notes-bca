  16. Rio Summit & Subsequent International Efforts.
    • → UNCED: United Nations Conference on Environmental Development. Also Known As Rio-Summit.
    • → Was held at Rio-De-Janerio.
    • → From June 3 1992 to June 4 1992.
    • → 179 Countries had participated.
    • → 21 Agendas were declared.

    • Q. Write short note on Rio-Summit.

      • The Rio Summit (1992) was a major UN conference in Brazil that focused on global environmental issues and sustainable development. Key outcomes included:

      Rio Declaration: A set of principles for sustainable development.
      Agenda 21: An action plan for sustainable development in the 21st century.
      Conventions on Climate Change and Biodiversity: International agreements to address these critical issues.

      The Summit aimed to balance economic growth with environmental protection, a concept that remains highly relevant today.
